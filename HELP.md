Validation:
* You can't creat student and instructor with the same email or phone number, this data are unique.
* First name and second name must have more than 2 character and less than 20.
* Email address must comply with pattern like: kowalski@wp.pl.
* Phone number must must have 9 character and include only digits. 
* School official name must be unique.
* Registration car number must by unique and more than 4 characters and less than 9.
